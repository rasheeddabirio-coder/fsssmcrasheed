About Our Mosque

The Federal Site and Service Scheme Muslim Community Mosque (FSSSMC Mosque) serves as a spiritual, educational, and social center for Muslims and the wider community. It is a place of worship, learning, and unity where people of all backgrounds are welcome.

To be a leading Masjid that builds strong faith based on Allah-consciousness, piety, and mindfulness of Allah, serving as a spiritual shield against sins and wrongdoing.

To provide deep knowledge of the Qur'an and Sunnah of the Prophet (PBUH) through teaching and learning, provide a safe and holy environment for worship, and highlight the true values of Islam through regular, modern, and sustainable quality services for the care of Muslims in our community and beyond.

To live a life guided by the Qur'an and Sunnah as practiced by the Prophet (PBUH) and exemplified by the Sahaba.

The organisation structure of the Masjid is designed for smooth administration without undermining the authority of the Imam as the supreme spiritual authority according to the Sunnah of the Prophet (PBUH).

A) Spiritual Structure

Headed by the Imam.

B) Administrative Structure

Headed by the Amir.

Various committees and ad-hoc committees assist in implementing executive decisions and policies for effective programme and project execution.
